Me? Real.

I wrote my first code at 16. After teaching myself the basics of Python, I solved Project Euler's first math problem using my limited knowledge of operators and variables. I was instantly enthralled. When my family's small business (a fish farm) needed a software solution to calculate fish growth and feed rate, I knew I could help. While still in high school, I created and implemented an entirely new interface and tracking system using Excel VBA. Since then, I have continued to develop my skills in programming, learning Python, Excel VBA, R, and SQL. I am passionate about data analytics, computer science, and software engineering. With my computer science degree underway, I am excited to apply my passion for programming and data to the real world. P.S. - I'm also a real person! I love ultimate frisbee, disc golf, triathlons, reading, family, and fun :)
